Need Help Right Now?

Use these trusted starting points to find help with housing, utilities, food, healthcare, and other services in Chester County. If you're not sure where to begin, start with dialing 2‑1‑1.

2‑1‑1 (SEPA)

A free, regional social services hotline that connects you with human and social service resources in your neighborhood, supports coordinated entry for homelessness services, and guides you toward rent assistance options.

Call 2‑1‑1 Call 1‑866‑964‑7922

PA Benefits (COMPASS) & Local Office

Pennsylvania offers assistance programs for individuals and families in need. You can apply or renew online through COMPASS, or get help from caseworkers at the County Assistance Office.

Website: compass.dhs.pa.gov

Chester County Assistance Office
100 James Buchanan Drive, Thorndale, PA 19372‑1132
Hours: 8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.

Call Toll‑Free 1‑888‑814‑4698 Call 610‑466‑1000 Call LIHEAP 610‑466‑1042
